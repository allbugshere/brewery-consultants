Mr. Manpreet Singh Sandhu

Restaurant Designer & Concept Creator
Digital Marketer & Brand Strategist
Founder, House of Garden & Cavo Microbrewery
Project Management & F&B Operations Expert
The Leadership

Delivering Design, Branding & Operations Excellence

With a refined eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of the hospitality industry, Manpreet Singh Sandhu is a multifaceted professional who has carved a distinctive niche at the intersection of interior design, restaurant operations, and digital marketing. Based in Mohali, Punjab, Manpreet has successfully conceptualised, designed, and launched landmark hospitality projects across North India — from vibrant restaurant spaces and dental clinics to full-scale hotels nestled in the Himalayas.

His consultancy vision is built on a simple belief: every space must tell a story that keeps guests coming back. He offers end-to-end design, branding, and operational strategy to hotels, restaurants, and lifestyle venues across India.

Active Ventures

Owned & Operated

House of Garden
Mohali, Punjab
A garden-themed dining destination offering a lush, immersive experience that blends nature-inspired interiors with carefully curated cuisine. Designed and developed by Manpreet, this restaurant has become a recognized landmark in Mohali's dining scene, celebrated for its unique aesthetic and warm hospitality.
Cavo Microbrewery
Sector 66, Mohali, Punjab
A premium microbrewery and dining venue that fuses craft beer culture with an upscale, contemporary design philosophy. Cavo represents Manpreet's vision of creating spaces where people gather, celebrate, and connect — backed by a live brewing experience and dynamic food menu.
